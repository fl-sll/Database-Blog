Problem Statement

Sometimes, organising an event could be quite challenging. Many problems may arise such as understaffing, overspending, schedule changes, and many more. With our database system, human errors can be thrown out of the equation, making sure that everything runs smoothly by reviewing the most common event planning problems and giving solutions that will help you be more safe than sorry.

Target User

Event Organisers
